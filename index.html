üìñ About This Project

This project provides three different interfaces to explore the same comprehensive Bible cross-reference dataset from the Treasury of Scripture Knowledge. Whether you prefer reading in the terminal, exploring visual patterns in the browser, or analyzing with professional 3D graphs, there's an interface designed for you.

Data Source: All cross-references come from the Treasury of Scripture Knowledge, a public domain collection of 340,000+ verse connections compiled by scholars over centuries. Bible translations include KJV, ASV, WEB, and YLT - all public domain.

Perfect For: Personal Bible study, sermon preparation, academic research, teaching presentations, discovering thematic connections, and exploring the interconnected structure of Scripture.
